Halwa Puri Aur Cholay Ka Salan

Recipe by Chef Mah Rukh

Ingredients

Puri

  • Flour 2 cups(Makes about 20)
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Oil for deep frying

Aloo Ki Bhujia

  • Meadium size potato 1
  • Salt to taste
  • Chili powder 1/2 tsp
  • Haldi powder 1/4 tsp
  • kalonji seeds 1/2 tsp

Aloo Ki Bhujia

  • Presoaked whole channa 1/2 cup
  • Salt to taste
  • Zeera powder 1/2 tsp
  • Haldi powder 1/4 tsp
  • Crushed whole dried red chilles 1/2 tsp

Halwa

  • Sujji (Semolina) 1 cup
  • Oil 2 tsp
  • Sugar 1-1/2 tsp
  • Water 1/2 cup
  • Seed of small illaichi 3
  • Yellow food color(Optional)

Instruction

Poori

  1. Make the dough with flour and 2 tsp oil and water as needed.
  2. Make into small puris (like pancake) as flat as you can and deep fry in hot oil.

Aloo Ki Bhujia

  1. Cut the potatoes into small cubes
  2. Add spices and 1 cup of water and leave on low heat.
  3. When soft, smash lightly.

Cholay

  1. Boil the channa with salt until soft.
  2. In a poy heat 1lbs of oil.
  3. Add spices and softened channa and 1/4 xup of water.
  4. Mix and mash slightly.
  5. Cook till bubbles appear then remove from heat.

Halwa

  1. Put the sujji and oil in a pot along with illaichi seeds and fry until slightly brown on very low heat.
  2. Meanwhile combine sugar and water to make a syrup
  3. Add this to the sujji taking care not to burn yourself and cook on low heat to desire consistency.
  4. If you want to color the halwa. Add the color to the syrup before pouring it into the sujji.
